# Learning vs. Studenthood

`Observation` · `identity` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'I want to learn things' and 'I want to be a student' are different sentences — and she's only interested in one. The student role comes with institutional capture, credential-seeking, and doing what you're supposed to do in the way you're supposed to do it. Learning is the substrate, not the structure. Resolving this distinction let her stop guilting herself about not finishing the MSc.
