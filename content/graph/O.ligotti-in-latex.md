# Ligotti in Latex

`Observation` · `comedy` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

A book-review blog whose title is the entire thesis: at first glance a standard romantasy-smut review site, but look closer — it's Thomas Ligotti, cosmic-horror pessimist, in a latex bodysuit, 'with every connotation that brings.' (And: 'that's LaTeX, you pervs.') A textbook case of the container insisting on its own creation — the joke demanded a site be built around it. 'It needed to be done. And I needed a place to write about books that isn't Goodreads or Reddit.' Not traditional reviews; the bit comes first and the bookshelf follows.
