# Attention Weighting

`Concept` · `vector_semantics` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

The mechanism in transformer architecture by which the model decides which tokens in context are most relevant to interpreting the current token. Related to but not identical to her intuition about weighting high-context vs low-context words. The key innovation of transformers over earlier models: earlier models had static word vectors, transformers produce dynamic contextual ones. Same word, different neighborhood depending on context.
