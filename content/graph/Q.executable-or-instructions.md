# Executable, or Instructions?

`Question` · `hci` · *unverified_claim* · visibility: **publishable**

Genuinely unresolved: 'should that be shared as an executable file, or should it exist as I told Claude what to do, and you can too?' If software is becoming inane and increasingly vibe-coded, the artifact worth distributing may be the instruction, not the binary. Left open on purpose — the question is more load-bearing than either answer.
