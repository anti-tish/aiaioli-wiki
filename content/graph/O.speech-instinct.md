# The Speech Instinct

`Observation` · `writing` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'I was idly thinking about how you would introduce those two on a panel, how would you highlight their more obvious differences and tie those into their shared values. And then I was like — oh, this is a speech, just not for any audience lol.' The speech was written before she noticed she was writing it. A specific cognitive mode: finding the connective tissue between two people's public identities and making that tension useful for an audience. The observation was that speechwriting is her natural register, separate from the essay or the book.
