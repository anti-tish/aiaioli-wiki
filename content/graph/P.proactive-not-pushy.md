# Proactive, Not Pushy

`Pattern` · `ai_design` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

The receiving-human methodology stated as a design constraint: the right move isn't 'Claude, write a blog post on X,' it's the AI noticing 'hey, you mentioned this in a few different ways, here's what you said — maybe tie it together,' because that takes the least translation. The hard part is being proactive without being a professional nag: 'being a professional nag is incredibly hard, which is why Clippy will forever live in infamy.' Her sketch of the non-annoying version: an unobtrusive 'see related notes' that's constantly running but trivially easy to ignore — the opposite of an interruption.
