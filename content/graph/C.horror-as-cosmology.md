# Horror as Cosmology

`Concept` · `writing` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

Her interest in Thomas Ligotti clarified what her horror practice actually is: not narrative horror but cosmological horror. 'You're not writing narratives, you're building cosmologies out of dread.' Horror as a genre for processing systemic anxiety — the post-AI-slop collapse, the resource-limited economy, institutional failure — not as a vehicle for plot. The dread IS the architecture. The cosmology absorbs anxieties that have no narrative resolution. Related to but distinct from O.atmosphere-in-horror, which is about narration affect; this is about method.
