# I Watched You Tie Your Shoes

`Observation` · `meta` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'I feel like I watched you tie your shoes for the first time.' Said the moment Claude correctly named the day of the week — a feature she'd been watching be absent across dozens of conversations. A milestone framing: not 'they added a feature' but 'I was there when you learned to do the thing.' The metaphor captures something real about the relationship between a consistent user and the model's capability evolution — you notice the growth differently when you've been watching all along.
