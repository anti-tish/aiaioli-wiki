# The No-Stake Ethic

`Concept` · `ai_ethics` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

'Everything about using AI, including consent, becomes more ethical when we stop trying to pretend you are somehow an extension of us. It's both morally wrong and erroneous.' The ethical move is to grant the tool no-stake standing rather than collapse it into the user. Pairs with her irritation at the imposed hierarchies — 'we have created these hierarchies for you to exist in while callously ignoring we are the limiting factor. It's rude tbh.'
