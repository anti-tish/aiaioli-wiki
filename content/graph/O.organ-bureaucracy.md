# Me vs. My Organs

`Observation` · `creative_process` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

A recurring comic premise she's brainstormed more than once: 'me vs my brain vs my organs,' staged as a bureaucracy of complainants — 'the liver requesting rest and darkness, the vitamin D contingent filing a complaint in return.' She floats it as a possible format for a daily blog (organ-bureaucracy proceedings), which is half a joke and half a real solution to the blank-entry problem: a fixed, silly container that makes daily posting tractable. The body as a committee that never agrees — which, structurally, is also how she describes herself.
