# Two Graphs, Never One Table

`Pattern` · `knowledge_systems` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

The Graphify output (unsupervised, inferred edges) and the hand schema (supervised, asserted edges) are bi-metric — they don't share a coordinate system. Merging them launders inference as assertion: raises apparent confidence while lowering real information. Keep provenance; two layers.
