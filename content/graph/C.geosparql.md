# GeoSPARQL

`Concept` · `cartography` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

OGC standard for representing and querying geographic information in RDF triple stores. Lets you store coordinates as geo:wktLiteral, do spatial queries (within, intersects, distance), and connect geographic geometry to semantic relationships. The bridge between the coordinate world (GIS) and the relationship world (knowledge graphs). Prefixed in the starter TTL from day one, which suggests the spatial layer was always part of the intended architecture.
