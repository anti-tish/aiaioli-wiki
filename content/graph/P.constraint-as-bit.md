# Constraint as Bit

`Pattern` · `comedy` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'I have put fake limitations on myself in the name of the bit. Classic Tish.' She deliberately chose the free .wordpress.com plan — with the publicly visible wordpress.com in the URL — because she found it aesthetically and comedically appropriate. Not a real constraint; a chosen one, kept because it's funnier. The broader pattern: adopting arbitrary constraints for aesthetic or comedic reasons, then honoring them as if they were real. The bit creates the limitation. The limitation becomes the work.
