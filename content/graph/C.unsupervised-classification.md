# NLP Training as Unsupervised Classification

`Concept` · `computational_linguistics` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

Training a language model is unsupervised classification at scale — relatedness inferred from co-occurrence the way k-means infers clusters from spatial proximity. Nobody labeled the neighborhoods. So the clusters encode a social signal (how humans talk about things together), not a physical one. Arrived at independently from a GIS background.
