# Off-Grid Mesh / Resilient Comms

`Concept` · `platform_studies` · *hunch* · visibility: **publishable** · extraction: **hand**

pocket node. The appeal isn't gadgetry; it's the off-ramp from corporate-owned infrastructure: comms
that keep working when (or because) the platforms don't. Sits at the intersection of FOSS values,
collapse-preparedness, and the dark-forest instinct. Captured the moment she almost bought a T-Deck.
