# A Book I Could Have Written

`Observation` · `criticism` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

Her nominee for the single most devastating criticism available: 'the meanest criticism I could ever give someone: this is a book I could have written.' Aimed, with relish, at a particular qntm book that's been a thorn in her side. It lands because it withholds the one thing an author wants — to have made something only they could make — and does it without raising its voice. Precision over volume; the insult is the absence of awe.
