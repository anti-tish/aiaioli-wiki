# The Bubba / Stranger Things Edge

`Pattern` · `knowledge_systems` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

The canonical honest-messy edge. The graph linked her dog Bubba to Stranger Things 4, and the link is real but trivial: a single Diarly entry that says, in effect, 'I have the cutest dog in the world and Stranger Things 4 is still good.' Same-day, same-entry adjacency, not conceptual kinship — the exact artifact of journaling one document per day and timestamping unrelated thoughts. The example that defines what a weak co-occurrence edge looks like, and why the journal graph needs a lighter schema than the chats.
