# Housing as Network Topology

`Concept` · `geography_cartography` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

Her apartment search, reframed: not fixed point-locations but a network. 'Think of it as a route/network topology — it can be any shape between the various nodes, but the lengths (in distance or time) between nodes have to be within a certain window.' Home, social hub, workspace, and the rest are nodes; what matters is the edge-weights, not the coordinates. A geographer treating her own life as a graph with a tolerance on path length.
