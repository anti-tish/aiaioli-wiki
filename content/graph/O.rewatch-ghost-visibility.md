# Rewatch Ghost Visibility

`Observation` · `writing` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'Re-watching Haunting of Hill House the first time after the initial watch is SO fucking scary. Seeing all the ghosts you didn't notice the first time is just tremendously awful.' The horror of retrospective visibility: the information was always there; you just didn't have the key. The rewatch is scarier than the first watch because now you can see what was hiding. An aesthetic principle as much as a viewing experience — design something where the second read is the more unsettling one.
