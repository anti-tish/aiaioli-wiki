# Pay First, Then Ask

`Observation` · `social_tactics` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

A social-access tactic she names while planning to break into leatherwork: bring a craftsperson actual paying repair work first, then ask for their professional opinion — 'people are always more receptive when you're paying them money than asking for their professional opinion' for free. The foot in the door isn't charm, it's the transaction; respect the expertise by buying it before you borrow it. A small, repeatable maneuver for getting close to people who know things.
