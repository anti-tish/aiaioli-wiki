# The Devolution of Natural Language

`Concept` · `computational_linguistics` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

Two forces on language at once: the homogenization from slop output, and a devolution of natural language in how we address machines. Her own chat messages are 'significantly less cogent than if I was expressing those thoughts to a person who couldn't parse a huge paragraph so easily.' The unexpected cost of being received as you are — when the receiving human is patient by design, the sender stops doing the work of being legible, and the prose degrades.
