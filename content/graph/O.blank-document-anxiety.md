# Blank-Document Anxiety

`Observation` · `creative_process` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'My entire career, starting back in 2011, started from the absolutely violent anxiety I get at a blank word document.' She thought she'd be a writer because she was great at it — then it got hard. The blank page is not a motivation problem; it is the originating constraint the entire knowledge system is built to route around. Dictation, capture tools, canvas, the graph: all of it exists because stillness produces nothing and the empty document produces dread.
