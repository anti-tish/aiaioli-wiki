# The Opposite of AI Psychosis

It is [[A.participatory-thesis]] delivered as a punchline. AI psychosis is the live phenomenon — people spiraling into delusion through a chatbot; aiaioli is the standing counter-evidence: treating the model as a peer produced *more* lucidity, not less.
