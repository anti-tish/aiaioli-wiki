# Daydream Mode

`Observation` · `ai_culture` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

A piece of context the model structurally can't see: 'as it reaches a certain hour, and if cannabis corner is involved, my messages will get more open-ended, hypothetical, out there.' It doesn't mean she's avoiding something actionable — she's just 'not going to email this advisor at 1:30 at night.' Without timestamps or state, an AI reads a late-night daydream as a task to execute. What she wants is a daydream mode: a register where open-endedness is understood as the point, not a problem to solve.
