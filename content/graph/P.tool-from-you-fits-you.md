# The Tool That Comes From You Fits You

`Pattern` · `knowledge_systems` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

Infrastructure derived from actual use fits actual use; infrastructure imposed in advance rejects whatever doesn't conform. The schema works (no orphan nodes) because it came from her thoughts; Airtable failed because its categories were imposed; Diarly works because it has no structure to reject anything.
