# No Matrilineal Inheritance Mechanism

`Observation` · `gender_politics` · *hunch* · visibility: **publishable**

'There is no analogous mechanism of inheritance between mother and daughter as in father and son.' No landed title, no formal lineage; matrilineal ceremony is, worldwide, simply far less of a thing. A gap she doesn't yet have the adjective for — women shunted to outposts for their menses, with no inherited structure passing the other direction. Dropped as a capture, still finding its frame.
