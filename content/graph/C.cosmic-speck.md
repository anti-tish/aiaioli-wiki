# Cosmic Speck

`Concept` · `identity` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'My only "real" fixed point is that of a speck of dust on a barely larger speck. That's the only thing I know to be "true." Which means I might have considerably more importance placed on maximizing the fuck out of being that speck.' Nihilism inverted: cosmic insignificance as permission, not despair. If nothing you do changes the scale of the universe, then the only variable is what you make of your speck. A Hitchhiker's Guide formulation built out in childhood and never abandoned. Anti-earthly-divinity; anti-assigned-meaning; pro-maximizing-the-speck.
