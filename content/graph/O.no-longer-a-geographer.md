# No Longer a Geographer

`Observation` · `identity` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'I don't feel like a geographer anymore as in career or area studies. Obviously my cognitive profile remains exactly the same.' The field was load-bearing for her identity for over a decade. What she's dropping is the label, the institutional affiliation, and the GIS software — not the spatial cognition or the projection-problem epistemology. The geographer's way of seeing the world is non-negotiable; the job title was contingent.
