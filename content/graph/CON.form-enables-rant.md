# Form Enables the Rant

`Connection` · `writing` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

**From:** [[P.medium-is-antagonist]]
**To:** [[O.remarkable-rant]]
**Relationship:** has_instance_in_corpus

The reMarkable rant is the pure, undiluted expression of P.medium-is-antagonist with an ironic twist: the tool's constraints became the content, and the hostility to the tool became the gift. A 7-part screed aimed AT a naggy Claude about a pen that keeps being called a stylus, a device that doesn't produce PNG deliverables, a company that wants everything to be e-ink documents — the medium fought back and lost, and the fight IS the piece. Tish rates it some of her funniest work. The constraint didn't block the content; it generated it. Form as antagonist demonstrating itself in the corpus it belongs to.
