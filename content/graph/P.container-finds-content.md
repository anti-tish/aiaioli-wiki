# The Container Waits for Its Content

`Pattern` · `creative_process` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

Her sequencing rule, applied to the long-dreaded MSc thesis: 'I'm going to pursue the things that are loudly calling my name. If during the process the right story for that container comes along, I will write it.' The thesis stops being an obligation to fulfill and becomes a container that gets filled if and when the matching content shows up. Detaching from a decade of thesis anxiety is reframed not as giving up but as 'the ultimate act of creating meaning: detaching from the old one.'
