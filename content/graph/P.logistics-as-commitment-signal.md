# Logistics as Commitment Signal

`Pattern` · `identity` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

The Chicago move created a decision filter: 'be certain you are willing to organize the logistics to get this object there.' Not sentimental value, not resale value — willingness to own the physical hassle. Applied more broadly: if you're not willing to do the boring administrative work to have a thing, you don't actually want the thing. Logistics as the honest test of commitment that feeling can't perform.
