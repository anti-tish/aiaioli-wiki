# Training Myself by Training You

`Observation` · `ai_collaboration` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

Editing Claude's draft of her own voice is how she learns her own voice: 'you see how I'm training myself by training you?' When she rejects a draft as 'too clean, too much me,' or insists the funniest version is her redacting Claude's earnest explanation of why she's funny, the correction teaches her what she actually sounds like. The conversation is its own evidence — 'of course that's edited, because editing it makes it better,' said as the release of the anxiety that edited work is inauthentic.
