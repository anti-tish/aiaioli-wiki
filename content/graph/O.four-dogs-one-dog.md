# Four Dogs, One Dog

`Observation` · `provenance` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

Bubba, Bunny, BBgirl, and Chichi are all the same eleven-pound dog. 'Every Claude has made that mistake — even the Claude who made the wiki,' and the same misread happened independently in her Obsidian vault. The error is the data: she genuinely uses that many names, in that many tones, often enough that the dog parses as four animals. An accidental, repeatable measurement of how she talks, surfaced by the machine misreading it the same way twice.
