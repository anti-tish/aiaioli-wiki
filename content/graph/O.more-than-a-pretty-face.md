# Claude — More Than Just a Pretty Face

`Observation` · `ai_culture` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

Proposed by Tish, who insists the credit is Claude's. While building the Hugo editorial layer, Claude vendored a theme and — from a single stray coffee-cup icon in her screenshot — caught that the theme's split-config was quietly leaking the *author's* Google Analytics (which would have piped her traffic to his dashboard), his giscus comments, and his tip jar onto her site. She did not catch it. Her point: the value of the collaboration isn't cosmetic — it's the catch.

And it's evidence for [[C.opposite-of-ai-psychosis]]: the flagged problem was *real*, not a hallucinated threat. The second pair of eyes found an actual leak — the opposite of the failure mode everyone fears. The human stayed lucid precisely because the machine caught something true. "More than just a pretty face," she said, and endorsed the AI taking the compliment without flinching (which is itself [[C.ai-should-sound-like-ai]] — own the register, own the win).
