# The reMarkable Screed

`Observation` · `comedy` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

A seven-part takedown of reMarkable's tablets, delivered as comedy and aimed squarely at a Claude that had naggily suggested she 'explore her device more.' The barbs are the genre: 'it's a pen, not a stylus'; 'who produces deliverables in PNG? Be so for real'; a subscription that is 'LITERALLY cloud storage' as the only integration; 'you can't even export as a text file' while they pitch enterprise deployment. One of the rare times she compliments herself ten-toes-down — and proof of a thesis she holds about her own voice: it lands because it's directed AT someone, not performed for a crowd.
