# Bad Girls Double Standard

`Observation` · `culture` · *confident* · visibility: **publishable**

Amber Heard received intense public scrutiny and a full-media trial; Jeffrey Epstein's case was placed under total secrecy (protects his network). Sabrina Carpenter called overtly sexual; men who don't care about consent succeed. This isn't conspiracy — it's purity politics. The mechanism is the same one used to 'keep people at their station': the designation of 'bad' is applied to disqualify from defense and sympathy. Identical behaviors carry different social consequences depending on who performs them. The bad girls defense attorney frame emerges directly from this: the system punishes women for being human, then uses that punishment as evidence of badness, then uses badness to justify further punishment. The loop is the mechanism.
