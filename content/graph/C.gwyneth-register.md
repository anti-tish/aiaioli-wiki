# The Gwyneth Register (I Wish You Well)

`Concept` · `comedy` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **working**

The one person she is ironically parasocial over. Named not for the films but for a *register*: "so weird! so unbothered! so shamelessly hypocritical but in a mostly non dangerous way!" Utterly head in the clouds. Exhibit A: telling the man who sued her "I wish you well," politely, on her way out of court, in big thick-framed glasses — the courteous knife, the exit with no overpromise and no bed-shitting. Exhibit B: calling Timothée Chalamet "rock and roll" for dating a single mom, because she genuinely did not register that the single mom was Kylie Jenner — obliviousness so total it loops back to a kind of grace.

The aspirational pole of derisive-*dreamy*: the don't-pretend-to-promise woman who floats above the discourse she's technically in, unbothered to the point of weather system. The dreamy half, weaponized by sheer detachment. "I wish you well 🤓."
