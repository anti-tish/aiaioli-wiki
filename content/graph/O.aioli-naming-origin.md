# The Aioli Naming Origin

`Observation` · `meta` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

The name "AI Aioli" was coined in one of the first cross-session planning chats — and immediately earned its keep: 'good luck getting your text to voice to handle that one.' But the name came with the architecture: 'at the end of each thread, you create a summary that starts our next thread and we build on that. Kind of like 50 first dates lmaooooo.' The project named itself and designed its own persistence mechanism in the same conversation. Both are exactly as embarrassing to say aloud as they are apt.
