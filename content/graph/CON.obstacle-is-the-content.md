# The Obstacle Is the Content

`Connection` · `comedy` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

**From:** [[P.constraint-as-bit]]
**To:** [[P.medium-is-antagonist]]
**Relationship:** is_structurally_identical_to

P.constraint-as-bit: the limitation becomes the work; the bit creates the constraint and the constraint creates the material. P.medium-is-antagonist: the medium fights the narrator, and the fight IS the content. Both: resistance is generative. The difference is intentionality — in constraint-as-bit, she chooses the constraint deliberately (the free wordpress.com URL, kept because it's funnier); in medium-is-antagonist, the constraint exists and she finds the comedy in the fight. But the structure is identical: obstacle → content, constraint → bit. The slideshow fights the presenter. The version control infects the code. The .wordpress.com suffix stays in the URL. In every case, the thing that should be invisible has become the piece.
