# Writing, Not Writer

`Observation` · `identity` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'I recently accepted that I don't care about being a writer by profession. I care about having strong opinions and expressing them through writing. And those are mentally very different.' Writer = a job, a credential, an identity claim requiring external validation. Writing = the act of expressing strong opinions. Dropping the first doesn't threaten the second — it liberates it. Related to but distinct from the 'I am the writing' identification: that's about fusing self with text; this is about refusing the job title.
