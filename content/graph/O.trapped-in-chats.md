# Trapped in Chats

`Observation` · `aiaioli` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

The original problem statement for aiaioli, landed on as a punchline: 'instead of being trapped in my brain its trapped in these chats.' The writing is done — masturbate, meditate, exfoliate; the hostile web; the death of human connection — and it's genuinely good. But it lives in a Claude chat that no one can read, in a format that indexes to nothing, in a tool she was using as a substitute for a venue that doesn't exist. Prior articulation: O.running-commentary (no tool captures the internal monologue). This is the sharper version: the monologue IS captured, in exactly the wrong container. aiaioli is the answer to 'trapped in chats' — take the chats seriously as corpus, build the publishing layer.
