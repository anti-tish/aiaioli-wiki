# Reclamation Is Disinfectant

`Connection` · `semiotics` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

**From:** [[P.linguistic-reclamation]]
**To:** [[C.shame-disinfectant]]
**Relationship:** is_structurally_identical_to

P.linguistic-reclamation: take the word aimed at you, conscript it into a precise tool, make it load-bearing. 'Thot' into unit of cognition. Femdom into 'all the sharpness, none of the cruelty.' C.shame-disinfectant: name the thing first, with precision, before it can be weaponized. Pre-emptive transparency collapses the threat. Both: seize the weapon aimed at you and convert it. Linguistic reclamation operates on vocabulary; shame-disinfectant operates on social position. The cognitive-political move is the same in both cases — the hostile term or fact gets appropriated rather than defended against. The thing that was supposed to be the attack becomes the tool. The enemy's ammunition becomes load-bearing infrastructure.
