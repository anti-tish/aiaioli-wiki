# The Translation Gap

`Architecture` · `knowledge_systems` · *verified_fact* · visibility: **publishable**

Sending-to-computer and receiving-human are two distinct steps that never fully merge. Closeable through design, not solvable through collapse. The gap is the translation cost; the question is who pays it and when. (This is why the Graphify and hand-schema graphs must not be merged — they are two registers.)
