# The Now/Later Inbox

`Concept` · `knowledge_systems` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

Zero-friction capture distilled to a single decision: when you drop in a thought, the tool asks only 'deal with this now, or later?' Now opens a dialogue; later sends it to the inbox. The useful part is what the inbox then does — it recommends where the thought probably wanted to go, plus any other relevant connections, considering the entire inbox at once. A means of *emergently building the schema*, explicitly not Notion 2.0.
