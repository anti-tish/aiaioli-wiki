# Capture Friction: The Title

`Observation` · `tools` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'what i really want is like... a feed. where i can put things as i find them, and they dont need a title and save location etc. diarly is the closest thing ive found to this, but it is more for journaling and less for thought capture.' The specific failure of Apple Notes: the mandatory title field. Naming is not a trivial overhead — it is where the friction lives. A title requires categorization before the thought is understood; the thought-capture moment is precisely when you don't yet know what it is. This constraint, identified in Nov 2025, drove the discovery of Drafts as a title-free capture tool and ultimately the whole system architecture. See P.system-must-fit-not-teach for the broader principle; this is the specific failure mode that made it concrete.
