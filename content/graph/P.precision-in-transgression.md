# Precision in Transgression

`Pattern` · `comedy_performance` · *hypothesis* · visibility: **publishable**

The crime in the joke has to be pinpoint: 'I can't be shoplifting and expect a laugh from a bunch of people in sales.' Satire of 'a little light crime' only lands when the transgression is calibrated to exactly miss the audience's actual stakes. Same engine as her dialectics rule — the example must be precise enough that no one can dismiss it on a technicality. Imprecision is what gets a bit (or an argument) dismissed; precision is what makes it un-dismissable.
