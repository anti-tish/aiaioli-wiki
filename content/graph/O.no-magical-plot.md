# No Magical Plot

`Observation` · `writing` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

'There is no magical plot that will land in my skull that is somehow transformatively better than the ideas already in there.' Said as a corrective to the waiting-for-inspiration trap. The ideas she has are not the placeholder ideas; they are the ideas. The block isn't the absence of good material — it's a relationship to the material she already owns. Connects directly to the unknotting reframe: the work is excavation, not invention.
