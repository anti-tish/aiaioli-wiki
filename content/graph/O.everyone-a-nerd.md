# Everyone's a Nerd About Something

`Observation` · `semiotics` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

Fieldnotes from a gem-and-mineral show, 'the most incredible cross collection of weirdos' under one roof: the guy who digs his own fossils in Arkansas, the one openly narrating his Thailand-wife plan to the woman standing next to him, the one who 'secretly owns a vape and pharmaceutical facility' (and can therefore produce synthetic gemstones). Her takeaway is structural, not mean: 'everyone is for sure a neeeeeerd about something' — faceting, excavating, or just the money, 'but boy do they take it seriously.' She gravitates to the fanatics and is genuinely inspired by people who don't give a fuck.
