# Tish-isms

`Concept` · `semiotics` · *personal_truth* · visibility: **publishable**

A named class of her own output: precise observations of human semiotics in the world, too specific to call mere 'signs.' The canonical example — frozen pizzas 'shelved spine-out like books,' with the cheapest ones in bare shrink-wrap dumped on the bottom shelf: 'there are opinions about pizza, and then there is coming up with a new optimal way to pack as much pizza as possible.' She posts these to realpieceoftish; the precision is half the joke, which is why 'sign' is too generic a word for them.
