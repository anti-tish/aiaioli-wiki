Why this website is written the way it is and contains what it does.

The voice is not decoration on the ideas; it's the shape of them. Slapstick before elevated
([[O.slapstick-before-elevated]]) — the elevation is icing, the slapstick is load-bearing. Her
own name for the welded pair is [[C.high-brow-low-life]]: the joke is the vehicle, the critique
is the cargo, and the vehicle is naff *on purpose*, because a joke gets through a door an essay
can't. The technique underneath is precision: the funniest available sentence and the most
accurate one are usually the same sentence, aimed ([[CON.knife-is-the-bit]] — the knife is not
*in* the bit, the knife *is* the bit). And the humor is post-ironic in the strict sense
([[C.post-humor]]): the joke is fully meant.

It has rules. Name the shameful thing first, precisely, before anyone can use it as leverage —
disinfection by daylight ([[C.shame-disinfectant]]; the standing example of practical
self-help delivered as a tricolon is [[O.masturbate-meditate-exfoliate]], which is genuinely
better advice than the wellness industry's version). The message does not change with the room
([[P.message-invariant-to-audience]]) — which reads as hostility only if you expect an audience
to be catered to ([[P.hostile-to-audience]]: the friction is the form). Demands to formalize are
mostly enforcement wearing clarity's coat ([[C.decorum-as-policing]]), and the register's
darkest self-description is also its funniest: she is the bear that eats language and screams it
back transformed ([[C.bear-that-eats-language]]) — the monster claimed on purpose, before it
could be used on her.
